Finding the right pair of wireless headphones can be overwhelming with so many options on the market. I've spent the last month testing the most popular models to help you make the right choice.

## What We Look For

When evaluating headphones, I focus on three key areas:

1. **Sound Quality** - Clarity, bass response, and soundstage
2. **Noise Cancellation** - How well it blocks outside noise
3. **Comfort & Battery** - Can you wear them all day?
